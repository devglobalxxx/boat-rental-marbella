By Andra Kiirkivi Founder & CEO · Boat Rental Marbella · Updated 20 May 2026 · Reviewed by local Marbella skippers

One of the most common questions we get from guests is: Are towels and linens included on a Marbella yacht charter? The short answer is yes—towels are provided on every Boat Rental Marbella charter, but bed linens are not. This guide explains exactly what you can expect, so you can pack smart and enjoy your day on the Mediterranean without any surprises.

What’s Included in Every Charter

Every Boat Rental Marbella charter comes with a comprehensive all-inclusive package. For all boats, from the Astondoa 40 (€749/2h) to the Mangusta 80 (€4,719 minimum 4h), you get:

  • Licensed skipper
  • Fuel
  • Water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, and cava
  • Light snacks (e.g., crisps, olives, nuts)
  • Insurance and safety equipment
  • Spanish IVA (21% VAT)
  • Towels (standard bath-size)

Towels are included at no extra cost. You don’t need to bring your own unless you prefer a different type or size.

Are Bed Linens Provided?

No, bed linens are not provided on any of our boats. Our charters are day trips only—typically 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours—so overnight stays are not offered. If you are planning a longer excursion that involves sleeping aboard, you would need to arrange linens separately. However, we recommend booking a hotel on the Golden Mile or in Puerto Banús for overnight accommodation.

What Type of Towels Are Included?

The towels we provide are standard bath-size (approximately 70 cm x 140 cm). They are clean, fresh, and professionally laundered between every charter. They’re perfect for drying off after a swim in the Mediterranean or for a quick freshen-up. If you want a larger towel for sunbathing on the deck or a beach towel for the sand, we suggest bringing your own.

Should I Bring My Own Towel?

While we supply towels, many guests prefer to bring their own for specific reasons:

  • Beach towels: If you plan to stop at a beach like Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, a larger towel is more comfortable.
  • Microfiber towels: These dry quickly and pack small, ideal for day trips.
  • Personal preference: Some guests like a thicker, softer towel.

Ultimately, it’s your choice. We have you covered either way.

Packing Checklist for Your Marbella Yacht Charter

To make your day stress-free, here’s what we recommend packing:

  • Swimwear and a cover-up
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+) and lip balm with SPF
  • Sunglasses and a hat
  • Light jacket or windbreaker (the sea breeze can be cool)
  • Camera or waterproof phone case
  • Personal medication (e.g., seasickness tablets)
  • Your own towel if you prefer (optional)

Remember, we provide towels, drinks, and snacks, so you don’t need to bring those.

Why Towels Are Included on Our Charters

We include towels because we want your experience to be as seamless as possible. When you step aboard at Puerto Banús, you should be able to relax immediately without worrying about forgotten items. It’s part of our commitment to hassle-free luxury. Other details like the licensed skipper and fuel are also included, so you can focus on enjoying the Marbella coastline, from Estepona to Sotogrande.

What About Other Amenities?

Beyond towels, our boats come equipped with everything you need for a comfortable day: a shaded flybridge, sun pads, a galley, and a sound system. On the Mangusta 80, you even get a free jet ski. For pricing on other boats like the Azimut 58 Flybridge or Ferretti 94, please contact us via WhatsApp for a custom quote.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are towels included on a Marbella yacht charter?

Yes, towels are included on every Boat Rental Marbella charter. We provide clean, standard bath-size towels for each guest. They are part of the all-inclusive package, which also covers a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and Spanish IVA (21%).

Are bed linens provided on overnight charters?

No, bed linens are not provided on any of our boats. Our charters are day trips only (typically 2-8 hours), so overnight stays are not offered. If you plan to sleep aboard, you would need to arrange linens separately, but we recommend booking a hotel on the Golden Mile instead.

Can I bring my own towel on a boat charter?

Absolutely. While we supply towels, you are welcome to bring your own beach towel, oversized towel, or microfiber towel if you prefer. Many guests bring a second towel for sunbathing on the deck or for drying off after swimming in the Mediterranean.

What size are the towels provided?

The towels are standard bath-size (approximately 70 cm x 140 cm). They are suitable for drying off after a swim or for general use on board. If you want a larger towel for lounging on the sundeck, we recommend bringing your own beach towel.

Are there any extra costs for towels?

No, there are no extra costs. Towels are included in the charter price, which also covers the skipper, fuel, drinks, snacks, insurance, and taxes. You will not be charged any hidden fees for towels or linens.

Do I need to pack anything else for a yacht charter?

We recommend packing sunscreen (SPF 50+), sunglasses, a hat, swimwear, a light jacket or windbreaker (the sea breeze can be cool), and a camera. Towels are provided, but bring your own if you prefer a specific type. Also, remember any personal medication or a waterproof phone case.

What if I forget my towel?

No problem at all. We have towels on board for every guest. Just let your skipper know when you board, and they will provide one. You can also purchase a towel at the shops in Puerto Banús if you want a souvenir.

Are towels cleaned between charters?

Yes, all towels are professionally laundered between each charter. We maintain high hygiene standards, so you can expect fresh, clean towels every time. The same applies to the galley equipment and seating areas.
