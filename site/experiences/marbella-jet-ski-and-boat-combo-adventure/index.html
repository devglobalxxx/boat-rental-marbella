Why Choose a Jet Ski and Boat Combo in Marbella?

Combining a jet ski with a boat charter gives you the best of both worlds: adrenaline and relaxation. You'll zip along the coast on a jet ski, then retreat to a spacious yacht for sunbathing, swimming, and drinks. It's perfect for groups who want variety without switching operators. Our combos depart from Puerto Banús, the heart of Marbella's luxury scene, and cover hotspots like Cala del Faro and Cala Cortés. With a licensed skipper handling the boat, you can focus on fun.

What's Included in the Combo?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). The jet ski session is guided by our crew, with life jackets provided. You don't need a license—just bring your sense of adventure. For the Mangusta 80, the jet ski is complimentary on 4-hour bookings. For other yachts, we'll arrange the jet ski at an additional cost.

Sample Itinerary for a 4-Hour Combo

A typical 4-hour combo might look like this: Depart Puerto Banús at 10:00, cruise east towards Cala del Faro. After 30 minutes, stop for a jet ski session (30–45 minutes) near the cliffs. Then anchor for swimming, drinks, and snacks. Head back via the Marbella coastline, passing the Golden Mile. Arrive at 14:00. For longer charters, we can reach Estepona or Sotogrande. Best Spots for Jet Skiing Near Marbella

The Costa del Sol offers excellent jet ski conditions year-round. Popular spots include:

  • Cala del Faro – Rocky coves and clear water, 15 minutes from Puerto Banús.
  • Cala Cortés – A quiet bay ideal for jet ski jumps and photo ops.
  • Marbella coastline – Ride past the Golden Mile and La Concha mountain backdrop.
  • Estepona – Further west, with longer stretches of open water.

Our skipper will guide you to the best spots based on sea conditions and your group's preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a Marbella jet ski and boat combo?

Our combo includes a private boat charter with a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks (water, soft drinks, beer, cava), light snacks, insurance, and IVA. Plus, you get a jet ski session (typically 30–60 minutes) guided by our crew. The jet ski is complimentary on the Mangusta 80 for a 4-hour minimum booking.

How much does a jet ski and boat combo cost in Marbella?

The Mangusta 80 combo starts at €4,719 for a 4-hour charter, which includes a free jet ski. For other boats, pricing is on request—contact us via WhatsApp for a custom quote. All prices include skipper, fuel, drinks, snacks, and taxes.

Which boats can I use for a jet ski and boat combo?

The Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) is our dedicated combo yacht with a free jet ski. Other vessels like the Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) or Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax) can also accommodate a jet ski—just ask us. All depart from Puerto Banús.

Do I need a license to ride the jet ski?

No, you don't need a license. Our licensed skipper will provide a safety briefing and guide you during the jet ski session. The activity is supervised, and life jackets are provided. You must be at least 18 years old to drive the jet ski.

How long is the jet ski session?

The jet ski session usually lasts 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the charter duration and group size. Our crew ensures you have ample time to enjoy the thrill while exploring the coastline. Longer sessions can be arranged on request.

Can I combine the jet ski with other water toys?

Yes, many of our yachts offer additional water toys like paddleboards, snorkel gear, and inflatable floats. For the Mangusta 80, the jet ski is included, but you can add other toys at an extra cost. Contact us via WhatsApp for a custom package.

Where do we go during the combo charter?

You'll depart from Puerto Banús and can cruise to spots like Cala del Faro, Cala Cortés, or the Marbella coastline. The jet ski ride takes you closer to the shore for added thrills. Longer charters can reach Estepona or Sotogrande.

How do I book a jet ski and boat combo?

Booking is easy—contact us via WhatsApp or use our online form. For the Mangusta 80, book a minimum of 4 hours to get the free jet ski. For other boats, we'll provide a custom quote. Payment is secure, and we confirm your booking within 24 hours.
