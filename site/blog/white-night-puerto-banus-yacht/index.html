The White Night Puerto Banús (Noche en Blanco) is the Costa del Sol’s most glamorous all-night cultural event. Every summer, the marina transforms into a open-air stage with live music, DJ sets, art installations, and a spectacular fireworks display. But the best seat in the house isn’t on the dock – it’s on the water. Chartering a private yacht from Boat Rental Marbella gives you front-row views of the fireworks, your own drinks, and total freedom to enjoy the night your way.

What is White Night Puerto Banús?

Noche en Blanco is a European cultural phenomenon that started in Paris. In Puerto Banús, it’s an annual night-long festival featuring free concerts, street theatre, art exhibitions, and a grand fireworks finale over the marina. The event typically runs from early evening until 6am, with thousands of people flooding the Golden Mile and Puerto Banús. The dress code is white – hence the name – and the atmosphere is electric.

Why Watch from a Yacht?

Watching from a private yacht eliminates the crowds, the queues for drinks, and the struggle to find a good view. You can anchor just off the marina and watch the fireworks directly overhead. Our boats come with a skipper, so you don’t have to worry about navigation or parking. You control the music, the drinks, and the company. Plus, you can retreat to a quieter spot if the party gets too loud – or join the action whenever you like.

How to Book Your Yacht for White Night

White Night is one of the busiest nights in Puerto Banús, so early booking is essential. Contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, group size, and any special requests (e.g., catering, decorations). We’ll confirm availability and send you a booking link. A deposit secures your slot. For the best experience, we recommend a 4-hour charter from 9pm to 1am – you’ll catch the sunset, the party atmosphere, and the fireworks at midnight. Alternatively, a 2-hour charter from 11pm to 1am works if you’re on a tighter schedule.

What to Expect on the Night

Dress in white (or your favourite white outfit). Bring a light jacket – it can get cool on the water after midnight. The skipper will position the boat for optimal views of the fireworks. You can play your own music through the boat’s sound system. Drinks and snacks are included, but you’re welcome to bring your own food or decorations. The fireworks typically start around midnight and last 15-20 minutes. After that, the DJ sets continue until dawn – you can stay on the water or head back to the marina to join the party on land.

Other Tips for White Night Puerto Banús

  • Arrive at Puerto Banús early (8pm) to find parking – it gets very busy.
  • Bring cash for any on-land purchases, though most bars accept cards.
  • If you’re prone to seasickness, take a preventative tablet an hour before departure.
  • Book a return taxi in advance – Uber and taxis are scarce after 2am.
  • Check the official event dates on the Marbella tourism website – usually late June or early July.

Frequently asked questions

What is White Night Puerto Banús?

White Night (Noche en Blanco) is a cultural festival held annually in Puerto Banús, featuring free outdoor concerts, art installations, street performances, and a spectacular fireworks display. It runs from evening until dawn, turning the marina into a vibrant party hub.

Why watch White Night from a yacht?

A private yacht gives you front-row seats to the fireworks and DJ sets without the crowds. You can enjoy unlimited drinks, your own music, and the comfort of a luxury boat. Plus, you can anchor close to the action or find a quieter spot – your choice.

Which boats are best for watching the fireworks?

Any of our boats can position for great views, but the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 guests) offers a spacious flybridge and sun deck – ideal for a group. The Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 guests) also has excellent upper deck visibility. For smaller groups, the Pershing 46 (14m, 10 guests) is a stylish option.

How much does a yacht charter cost for White Night?

Prices start from €749 for 2 hours on an Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39 (includes skipper, drinks, snacks, and VAT). For a premium experience, the Mangusta 80 starts at €4,719 for 4 hours and includes a free jet ski. Other boats are priced on request – just WhatsApp us.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and safety equipment. Spanish IVA (21%) is also included. No hidden extras – you just bring your guests and enjoy.

Can I bring my own food or decorations for White Night?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and decorations. Many guests bring a white-themed picnic or birthday cake. Just let us know in advance so we can prepare the boat. We also offer catering options on request.

How do I book a yacht for White Night Puerto Banús?

Contact us via WhatsApp or email to check availability for your preferred date. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance as White Night is very popular. We’ll confirm the boat, time, and any special requests.

What time should I start my charter for White Night?

The fireworks usually start around midnight, so a 2-hour charter from 11pm to 1am works well. For a full evening, book from 9pm to 1am (4 hours) to enjoy the sunset, the party atmosphere, and the fireworks. We can also arrange longer charters if you want to stay until dawn.
