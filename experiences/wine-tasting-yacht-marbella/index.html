Marbella isn’t just about sun and sea—it’s also one of Spain’s most exciting wine destinations. A wine tasting yacht Marbella charter combines the luxury of a private boat with the rich flavours of Andalusia. Depart from Puerto Banús, sip fino sherry as the sun sets over La Concha, and anchor off Cala Cortés with a glass of Ronda red. We handle everything: the skipper, the drinks, the snacks, and the navigation. You just bring your palate.

Why combine wine tasting with a yacht charter?

Andalusia produces some of Spain’s most distinctive wines—from the dry, saline finos of Jerez to the bold, high-altitude reds of Ronda. Tasting these vintages on the water elevates the experience: the sea air sharpens your senses, and the views of the Costa del Sol provide a stunning backdrop. A Marbella yacht charter lets you explore coves, swim in crystal-clear water, and enjoy a curated wine flight—all without leaving the boat.

Our wine tasting packages

We offer two standard wine-tasting options, both fully customisable via WhatsApp:

  • Andalusian Sampler (€749/2h) – Includes 3 wines (fino, oloroso, Ronda red), olives, almonds, and manchego. Perfect for a short sunset sail from Puerto Banús.
  • Premium Tasting (€2,299/8h) – Full-day charter with 5 wines, Iberian ham, tapas, and a dedicated wine host. Includes swimming stops at Cala del Faro or Cabopino.

Both packages include a licensed skipper, fuel, soft drinks, beer, cava, and all taxes (21% IVA).

Which boat suits your wine-tasting group?

Our fleet departs from Puerto Banús. For intimate tastings, the Bandido (11.5m, 8 pax) or Red Tide (11m, 6 pax) offer a cosy, social setting. For larger groups, the Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) has a spacious saloon and flybridge perfect for wine service. All boats include shaded seating, a sound system, and a cooler for your bottles.

Andalusian wines you’ll sample

We rotate our selection based on season and availability, but expect these classics:

  • Fino (Jerez) – Dry, crisp, perfect with seafood. A classic Marbella apéritif.
  • Oloroso (Jerez) – Nutty, rich, pairs with aged cheese.
  • Ronda Tinto – Bold red from the Serranía de Ronda, notes of dark fruit and spice.
  • Málaga Dulce – Sweet dessert wine from Moscatel grapes, ideal with chocolate.

For a full tasting, we can arrange a sommelier-led session (€150 extra) that dives into the region’s wine history.

Itinerary suggestions for your wine-tasting charter

All charters depart from Puerto Banús. Here are three popular routes:

  • Sunset Wine Cruise (2-3h) – Sail west along the Golden Mile, anchor off Marbella Marina, enjoy a fino and tapas as the sun sets behind La Concha.
  • Cala del Faro & Tasting (4h) – Head east to Cala del Faro, swim, then taste Ronda reds and manchego on the bow.
  • Estepona & Sherry (6h) – Cruise to Estepona, anchor in the bay, sample fino and oloroso with Iberian ham. Return via Cabopino.

All our charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and 21% IVA. No hidden fees. The wine-tasting add-on simply upgrades your drinks selection to premium Andalusian vintages.

Frequently asked questions

What wines are included in the wine tasting yacht charter?

We feature a curated selection of Andalusian vintages, including fino and oloroso sherry from Jerez, reds from Ronda (e.g., Tinto Fino, Syrah), and white wines from Málaga. A local expert can guide the tasting on request.

Can I customise the wine selection?

Yes. Contact us via WhatsApp to request specific wines or a sommelier-led tasting. We can arrange premium bottles from Bodegas La Sangre (Ronda) or González Byass (Jerez) at an additional cost.

How long is the wine tasting charter?

Minimum 2 hours (€749). Most guests book 4-6 hours to visit a cove, enjoy the tasting, and swim. Full-day (8h) charters from €2,299 include a longer wine experience.

Which boats are best for a wine tasting group?

For small groups (5-8 pax), the Bandido or Red Tide are cosy. For up to 12 pax, the Azimut 58 Flybridge or Mangusta 80 offer spacious saloons and sundecks ideal for wine service.

Do you provide food to pair with the wines?

Yes. Light snacks (olives, almonds, manchego, Iberian ham) are included. For a full lunch, we can arrange tapas catering from local providers at an extra fee.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 48 hours before departure. Within 48 hours, we offer a credit for a future charter (subject to availability). Weather cancellations are fully refundable.

Can I book a private sommelier?

Absolutely. We can arrange a certified sommelier for an additional €150/3h. They will present Andalusian wine history, tasting notes, and pairings tailored to your group.
