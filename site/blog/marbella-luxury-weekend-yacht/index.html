Marbella Luxury Weekend is the Costa del Sol's most anticipated showcase of opulence, drawing superyachts, supercars, and VIPs to Puerto Banús each June. Whether you are a seasoned attendee or a first-time visitor, chartering a Marbella Luxury Weekend yacht offers an unmatched perspective: you can cruise past the floating displays, anchor near the marina action, and enjoy the Mediterranean sun in total privacy. Our fleet departs directly from Puerto Banús, putting you at the centre of the event from the moment you step aboard.

What Is Marbella Luxury Weekend?

Marbella Luxury Weekend is an annual multi-day event that transforms Puerto Banús into a glittering showcase of high-end yachts, luxury cars, designer fashion, and gourmet dining. The Showcase Days are the highlight, where the marina's docks become a catwalk for the latest superyachts and exclusive brands. Attracting influencers, celebrities, and luxury enthusiasts from across Europe, the event is a must-visit for anyone who appreciates the finer things in life. For those who want to experience it from the water, a private charter is the ultimate upgrade.

Why Charter a Yacht for the Event?

Viewing Marbella Luxury Weekend from a private boat offers several advantages. You avoid the crowds on the marina promenade, enjoy uninterrupted views of the superyacht displays, and can anchor in the bay for a swim between events. Our charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks, so you can focus on the spectacle. With boats ranging from the 6.4m Mariah SX21 to the 29m Ferretti 94, there is an option for every group size and budget. Prices start at €749 for a 2-hour charter on an Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39, both departing from Puerto Banús.

Best Itineraries During Showcase Days

A 2-hour charter is perfect for a quick lap of Puerto Banús and the Marbella coastline. For a longer escape, head west to Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés for a swim stop, then return to the marina in time for the evening events. A 4-hour charter lets you reach Estepona or Cabopino, while an 8-hour full-day trip can take you as far as Sotogrande. All routes offer stunning views of La Concha mountain and the Golden Mile. Discuss your preferences with our team when booking.

Frequently asked questions

What is Marbella Luxury Weekend?

Marbella Luxury Weekend is an annual event held in Puerto Banús, showcasing luxury yachts, supercars, fashion, and gastronomy. It typically spans several days in late June, with Showcase Days where visitors can explore high-end brands and vessels. The event attracts celebrities, influencers, and luxury enthusiasts from across Europe.

Can I charter a yacht during Marbella Luxury Weekend?

Yes, you can charter any of our boats during the event. We recommend booking well in advance as demand is high. Our fleet departs from Puerto Banús, placing you right at the heart of the action. Prices start at €749 for a 2-hour charter on an Astondoa 40, including skipper, drinks, and snacks.

Which boats are best for viewing the Showcase Days from the water?

For a group of up to 12 guests, the Mangusta 80 (24m) or Azimut 58 Flybridge offer excellent deck space and panoramic views. Smaller groups might prefer the Azimut 39 or Pershing 46. All boats depart from Puerto Banús, so you can cruise past the superyacht displays and marina events.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). There are no hidden fees. For Tier B boats like the Mangusta 80, a jet ski is also included free of charge for the minimum 4-hour booking.

How long should I charter a boat for the event?

A 2-hour charter is enough for a quick spin around Puerto Banús and the coastline. For a more relaxed experience, we recommend 4 hours to explore nearby spots like Cabopino or Estepona. Full-day charters (8 hours) allow you to reach Sotogrande or even cross to Gibraltar on calm days.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Our standard inclusions already cover a selection of drinks and light snacks. You are welcome to bring additional food or drinks, but please note that glass bottles are not allowed on board for safety reasons. We can arrange catering for longer charters upon request.

How do I book a yacht for Marbella Luxury Weekend?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp using the number on our website. Provide your preferred date, boat, and duration. We will confirm availability and send you a booking link. Payment is required in advance to secure the charter, especially during peak events like Luxury Weekend.

What is the weather like during Marbella Luxury Weekend?

Late June in Marbella typically sees temperatures around 28-32°C with calm seas and light breezes. Sea conditions are usually excellent for boating, with visibility often exceeding 10 nautical miles. We monitor forecasts closely and will advise if any changes are needed.
