Halloween in Marbella is not just about nightclubs and tapas bars—it is also the perfect excuse to take the party offshore. A Halloween yacht party Puerto Banús combines the thrill of the Mediterranean with costumes, cocktails, and a touch of spooky glamour. Whether you are planning a private celebration for friends or a themed corporate event, our fleet offers boats for every group size and budget. From the compact Dubhe (8m, 5 pax) to the majestic Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax), you can anchor near the iconic Puerto Banús marina or cruise along the Costa del Sol. All charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, snacks, and insurance—so you only need to bring your costume and your party spirit.

Why Choose a Halloween Yacht Party in Puerto Banús?

Puerto Banús is one of Europe’s most glamorous marinas, and its sheltered waters make it ideal for evening charters. October sea temperatures hover around 20°C, and the sunset at 19:30 adds a dramatic backdrop for a Halloween celebration. A yacht offers complete privacy, no queues, and the freedom to anchor at spots like Cala Cortés or Cabopino. You can also combine your party with a jet ski ride on the Mangusta 80 (€4,719 for 4 hours, jet ski included) or a relaxed cruise along the Golden Mile. The experience is fully customisable: bring your own decorations, choose your music, and even arrange a catered dinner.

Costume Ideas for Your Yacht Party

Marine-themed costumes work brilliantly on a yacht: pirates, mermaids, sailors, or even a classic ghost. Since space on deck can be limited, avoid bulky props or long trains that could trip you. Glow-in-the-dark accessories, face paint, and LED masks are safe and photogenic. For a group, consider a coordinated theme like The Addams Family or Pirates of the Caribbean. Remember to bring a change of clothes if you plan to swim—the sea is still swimmable in October.

What’s Included in Your Charter

Every booking includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and safety equipment. Spanish IVA (21%) is included in the prices shown. You can bring your own food and additional drinks; some boats have a fridge and cooler. For the Mangusta 80, a jet ski is included free for the 4-hour minimum charter. Decorations are welcome, but please avoid open flames or glitter that could damage upholstery.

Sample Itinerary for a Halloween Evening

Here is a typical 4-hour Halloween charter departing at 17:00:

  • 17:00 – Board at Puerto Banús, welcome drink and safety briefing.
  • 17:30 – Cruise towards Cabopino, passing the Golden Mile and Marbella Marina.
  • 18:15 – Anchor at Cala del Faro for swimming, photos, and costume reveal.
  • 19:00 – Sunset toast with cava and snacks.
  • 19:30 – Return to Puerto Banús, dropping anchor near the marina for a spooky backdrop.
  • 21:00 – Disembark, or extend the evening with a dinner booking at a nearby restaurant.

Longer charters can include stops at Estepona or Sotogrande.

Frequently asked questions

Can I decorate the yacht for Halloween?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own decorations. Most charter boats allow party decorations such as banners, balloons, and tableware, as long as they do not damage the interior. Please avoid open flames or glitter that can be difficult to clean.

What is the best boat for a Halloween party with 12 guests?

For a group of 12, the Mangusta 80 (24m), Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m), or Ferretti 94 (29m) are excellent choices. They offer ample deck space, comfortable seating, and a licensed skipper. The Mangusta 80 also includes a complimentary jet ski.

Are costumes required on a Halloween yacht party?

Costumes are not mandatory, but they are highly encouraged to enhance the spooky atmosphere. Many guests dress up in themes like pirates, ghosts, or movie characters. We recommend comfortable, non-slip footwear for safety on deck.

How long is a typical Halloween yacht charter?

Most charters last 2 to 8 hours. A 2-hour trip (€749 on Tier A boats) is great for a sunset cruise, while a 4-hour or 8-hour charter allows time to visit spots like Cabopino, Estepona, or Cala del Faro. Evening charters are popular for Halloween.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and safety equipment. Spanish IVA (21%) is included. Decorations and food catering can be arranged separately.

Can we play music on the yacht?

Yes, all our yachts have Bluetooth or auxiliary audio systems so you can play your own Halloween playlist. Please keep volume at reasonable levels out of respect for other boats and marine life.

What happens if the weather is bad on Halloween?

Your safety is our priority. If the skipper deems conditions unsafe, we will work with you to reschedule or offer a full refund. October evenings in Marbella are usually mild, but we monitor forecasts closely.

How do I book a Halloween yacht party?

You can book directly through our website or via WhatsApp for personalised advice. For boats with pricing on request, simply send a message with your preferred date, group size, and duration, and we will get back to you promptly.
