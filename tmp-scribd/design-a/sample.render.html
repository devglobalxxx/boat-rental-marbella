Section 01

Overview

The Astondoa 40 — known around the marina as “Fufi” — is the Spanish answer to the classic Mediterranean motor yacht. Built in Cádiz by the Astondoa shipyard, fitted out in teak and cream leather, she is most at home anchored off the Golden Mile for a long lunch and a swim.

At 12.5 metres she carries up to 9 guests in genuine comfort, with a licensed skipper and fuel included in every rate. Her local provenance is part of the charm: an Andalusian-built yacht, chartered from Puerto Banús, cruising the coastline she was designed for.

Section 02

On-Board Highlights

Fufi is laid out for slow afternoons on the water — sun on the flybridge, shade in the saloon, toys off the swim platform.

  • Flybridge with shaded bimini and second helm
  • Cream-leather saloon with table-for-six and air conditioning
  • Two private cabins for overnight charter
  • Spanish-built (Astondoa, Cádiz) — local provenance
  • Snorkel gear, inflatable donut & paddleboard on board

Section 04

Departure & Booking Information

Departure port: Puerto Banús, Marbella. Your skipper meets you at the berth 15 minutes before departure for a short safety briefing; boarding is step-free from the pontoon.

What to bring: swimwear, towels, sunscreen and sunglasses. Soft-soled shoes or bare feet on deck. Drinks and light snacks are already on board — you are welcome to bring your own food for longer charters.

Popular routes: a 3-hour swim stop off the Golden Mile, a half-day run to Cabopino with lunch at anchor, or the full-day cruise toward Estepona with the paddleboard and snorkel gear in the water.
