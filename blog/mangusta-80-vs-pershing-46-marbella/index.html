By Andra Kiirkivi Founder & CEO · Boat Rental Marbella · Updated 20 May 2026 · Reviewed by local Marbella skippers

When choosing between a Mangusta 80 vs Pershing 46 in Marbella, you're comparing two exceptional yachts that offer very different experiences on the Costa del Sol. The Mangusta 80 is a 24-metre open yacht built for space and speed, while the Pershing 46 is a 14-metre sport cruiser designed for agility and style. Both depart from Puerto Banús and come with a full crew, drinks, and insurance — but which one suits your day on the water? Here's our honest breakdown.

Size and Capacity

The Mangusta 80 (24m) comfortably accommodates up to 12 passengers, making it ideal for larger groups, family outings, or celebrations. The Pershing 46 (14m) takes up to 10 pax, offering a more intimate setting. If you're planning a party or a corporate event, the Mangusta 80 wins on space. For a romantic couple or a small group of friends, the Pershing 46 feels perfectly proportioned.

Performance and Speed

The Pershing 46 is renowned for its sporty performance and sleek lines — a true thoroughbred. The Mangusta 80, while larger, is no slouch and offers a smooth, fast ride thanks to its powerful engines. Both yachts are perfect for cruising from Puerto Banús to hotspots like Marbella, Cala del Faro, or Estepona. For exact speed specs, contact us via WhatsApp.

Onboard Experience

Both yachts include: licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). The Mangusta 80 goes a step further by including a jet ski free of charge — a huge bonus for thrill-seekers. The Pershing 46 offers a more streamlined, performance-oriented experience, perfect for those who value design and handling.

Best Routes from Puerto Banús

Both yachts depart from Puerto Banús, giving you direct access to the best of the Costa del Sol. Popular routes include cruising past the Golden Mile, anchoring at Cala Cortés, or heading west to Sotogrande. The Mangusta 80's size makes it better for longer trips, while the Pershing 46 is nimble enough for quick dashes to Cabopino or Marbella Marina.

Which One Should You Choose?

Choose the Mangusta 80 if you're a large group wanting space, a jet ski, and a full-day party vibe. Choose the Pershing 46 if you prefer a sportier, more intimate ride with a focus on performance. Both are top-tier options for a yacht charter in Marbella — it really comes down to your group size and style.
