Looking for the best boat in Marbella for a stag or hen weekend? The Bandido is a 11.5m motorboat that departs from Puerto Banús and takes up to 8 passengers. It's compact, private, and comes with a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks included. No hidden fees, no fuss – just a party on the water.

Why Bandido works for stag and hen groups

Stag and hen weekends are about celebrating with your closest friends. Bandido gives you a private space for up to 8 people – no strangers, no queues, no bar tabs. You get a dedicated skipper who handles navigation while you focus on the fun. The boat is 11.5m long, with plenty of deck space for lounging, dancing, or just soaking up the Marbella sun.

Departing from Puerto Banús, you're minutes from Marbella's best nightlife, beaches, and restaurants. After your charter, you can head straight to the Golden Mile or a nearby club. It's a seamless transition from sea to shore.

What's included – no surprises

Every Bandido charter includes:

  • Licensed skipper
  • Fuel
  • Water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava
  • Light snacks
  • Insurance and safety equipment
  • Spanish IVA (21%)

There are no extra charges for ice, glasses, or basic amenities. Just message us via WhatsApp for a quote – we'll confirm availability and price within hours.

Ideal itinerary for a party charter

A typical Bandido charter from Puerto Banús can include a cruise along the coast to Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés – two of Marbella's most scenic coves. You can anchor, swim, and enjoy your drinks. The skipper knows the best spots for a dip and can adjust the route based on your group's mood.

If you want a shorter trip, staying closer to Puerto Banús means you can still enjoy a couple of hours on the water before hitting the town. The boat is fast and nimble, so you cover ground quickly.

Bandido vs other boats for groups

While larger yachts like the Mangusta 80 can take 12 passengers, Bandido is better for smaller groups who want an intimate, no-frills party. It's also more affordable – pricing is on request, but you're not paying for space you won't use. Compare our full fleet to see what fits your group size and budget.

How to book Bandido for your stag or hen weekend

Booking is simple. Contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, duration, and number of guests. We'll send you a custom quote for Bandido. Once confirmed, you pay a deposit and the balance on the day. The skipper will meet you at Puerto Banús – just bring your ID and your party spirit.

For group sizes over 8, consider our other boats like the Azimut 39 (11 pax) or Astondoa 40 (9 pax). But for an 8-person stag or hen do, Bandido is the sweet spot.

Marbella: the perfect backdrop for a celebration

Marbella is famous for its nightlife, beaches, and luxury lifestyle. A boat charter adds a unique twist to any stag or hen weekend. You get privacy, flexibility, and a skipper who knows the coast inside out. From the views of La Concha mountain to the glamour of Puerto Banús, every moment feels special.

And because Bandido is based in Puerto Banús, you're right in the heart of the action. After your charter, you can walk to any of the marina's bars, restaurants, or clubs. It's the ultimate base for a weekend to remember.
