No. 01

Overview

Astondoa 40 — known to her crew as “Fufi” — is the Spanish equivalent of the classic Mediterranean motor yacht. Built in Cádiz by Astondoa, fitted out in teak and cream leather, she is never more at home than anchoring off the Golden Mile for a long lunch while La Concha rises behind the bay.

At 12.5 metres she carries up to nine guests with a licensed skipper at the helm and fuel in the tanks — both included in every charter. With a shaded flybridge above and two private cabins below, she is equally suited to a two-hour swim stop or a full day along the Marbella coast.

No. 02

On-board highlights

Fufi’s layout splits the day neatly between sun and shade: the flybridge for the cruise out, the saloon’s table-for-six when lunch arrives, and the bathing platform for everything in between.

  • Flybridge with shaded bimini and second helm
  • Cream-leather saloon with table-for-six and air conditioning
  • Two private cabins for overnight charter
  • Spanish-built (Astondoa, Cádiz) — local provenance
  • Toys on board: snorkel gear, inflatable donut & paddleboard
