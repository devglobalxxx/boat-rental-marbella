Why Choose a Yacht for Your Sunset Photoshoot in Marbella?

A yacht provides a mobile platform that changes with the light. As the sun sinks behind La Concha mountain, you can anchor off Cala del Faro or cruise along the Golden Mile. The reflection off the water creates a natural fill light, reducing harsh shadows. Unlike a studio, you get 360-degree views and the ability to move with the sun. Our boats are clean, well-maintained, and photogenic—sleek lines, teak decks, and white upholstery that pops against the blue sea. Plus, the skipper handles navigation, so you focus entirely on the shoot.

How to Plan the Perfect Sunset Photoshoot Timing

Golden hour in Marbella varies by season. In summer (June to August), sunset occurs around 21:30, so a 2-hour charter from 19:30 to 21:30 captures the best light. For spring and autumn, sunset is earlier—around 20:00—so adjust accordingly. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early at Puerto Banús to board and settle. The skipper can position the yacht facing west for uninterrupted sun views. If you want a longer session, consider 4 hours to include twilight shots and a post-sunset drink. For larger yachts like the Mangusta 80 (Grey), minimum charter is 4 hours.

Top Locations for Yacht Photoshoots Near Marbella

Our skippers know the coastline intimately. Popular spots include:

  • Puerto Banús entrance – with the iconic marina and luxury yachts as backdrop.
  • Cala Cortés – a quiet cove with calm waters, ideal for reflective shots.
  • Río Verde – near the Golden Mile, offering views of the Marbella Club.
  • Estepona coast – 10 NM west, with fewer boats and a pristine horizon.
  • La Concha mountain – visible from the sea, adds depth to wide-angle photos.

Each location offers unique lighting and scenery. Your skipper will adapt to your preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time for a sunset photoshoot on a yacht in Marbella?

The golden hour, roughly 30 minutes before sunset, offers the best light. In summer (June–August), sunset is around 21:30, so a 2-hour charter from 19:30 to 21:30 works perfectly. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early at Puerto Banús to board.

Which yachts are best for a photoshoot session in Marbella?

Our Astondoa 40 and Azimut 39 are ideal for small groups (up to 11 pax) at €749/2h. For larger groups, the Mangusta 80 (12 pax) offers a spacious flybridge and sleek lines, starting at €4,719/4h. All depart from Puerto Banús.

Do I need a professional photographer for the shoot?

No, you can bring your own camera or phone. However, if you want professional shots, we can recommend local photographers upon request. The yacht’s deck and sunset backdrop provide natural, flattering light without extra equipment.

What is included in the photoshoot yacht charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No hidden costs—just bring your camera and enjoy.

Can I bring props or additional equipment for the photoshoot?

Yes, you’re welcome to bring props like balloons, signs, or extra outfits. The skipper can help position the yacht for the best light. Just ensure items are safe and won’t damage the boat. Let us know in advance via WhatsApp.

How do I book a sunset photoshoot yacht in Marbella?

Contact us via WhatsApp or our website. For the Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39, book directly at €749/2h. For larger yachts like the Mangusta 80, pricing is on request. We’ll confirm availability and departure time from Puerto Banús.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of the shoot?

We monitor forecasts closely. If conditions are unsafe (e.g., high winds or storms), we’ll reschedule or offer a full refund. Light clouds can actually enhance sunset photos, so we often proceed unless it’s risky.

Can I combine the photoshoot with a dinner or party?

Absolutely. Many clients book a 4-hour charter to capture sunset and then enjoy a dinner cruise along the Marbella coastline. The Mangusta 80 or Azimut 58 Flybridge are perfect for this, with ample space for dining and dancing.
