Overview

The Astondoa 40 — known as “Fufi” — is the Spanish equivalent of the classic Mediterranean motor yacht. Built in Cádiz by Astondoa, fitted out in teak and cream leather, and at home anchoring off the Golden Mile for a long lunch.

At 12.5 metres with room for nine guests, she sits in the sweet spot for families and small groups: big enough for shade, a saloon and two private cabins, small enough to feel like your own boat. Every charter sails with a licensed skipper, and fuel is included in the price.

Departure & booking info

The Astondoa 40 departs from Puerto Banús, Marbella’s flagship marina. Exact berth details are sent with your booking confirmation — plan to arrive about 15 minutes before departure so the skipper can run the welcome briefing and get you under way on time.

Popular routes include a swim-stop anchorage off the Golden Mile, a coastal cruise towards Cabopino, or a sunset loop back into Puerto Banús. Your skipper will tailor the route to the day’s conditions and your group’s plans.
