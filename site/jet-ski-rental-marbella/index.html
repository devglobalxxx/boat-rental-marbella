What you can do on the water

The Marbella coast is the perfect playground for a one-hour Sea-Doo session. The 200 m coastal swim-zone rule applies, but outside it you have miles of open water along the Golden Mile and east towards Cabopino. Typical 1-hour itineraries:

  • Puerto Banús → Río Verde → back — quick tour past the marquee beach clubs (Nikki Beach, Ocean Club).
  • Puerto Banús → Marbella Marina → back — see the Golden Mile from the water, including Marbella Club and Puente Romano.
  • Combine with a yacht anchor stop — if you've booked a charter, anchor off Río Verde and let guests take turns on the jet ski for 10 minutes each.

The Sea-Doo experience

Modern Sea-Doo PWC are easy to ride — twist-grip throttle, lanyard kill switch, intuitive handlebars. The 10-minute on-the-dock briefing covers everything you need to know. You will:

  1. Throttle and brake. Right thumb forward to go, release to coast. Sea-Doos have an iBR brake-and-reverse system that makes docking much easier than older jet skis.
  2. Kill switch lanyard. Clipped to your life jacket. If you fall off, the engine stops automatically.
  3. The 200 m rule. Stay outside the swim-buoy line along every Marbella beach. Penalties are stiff and the Guardia Civil del Mar patrol.
  4. Wake awareness. Slow inside the Puerto Banús harbour mouth and when passing other boats — keep your wake low.

After the briefing you take the Sea-Doo out for a controlled lap of the marina with our staff watching, and from there you're on your own.

Frequently asked questions

How much is a jet ski rental in Marbella?

€250 for the first hour on a Sea-Doo personal watercraft, then €200 for each additional hour (€450 for 2 hours), including fuel for normal use, life jacket, on-the-dock briefing and insurance. Two-up (rider plus passenger) costs the same as solo.

Do I need a licence to ride a jet ski in Spain?

For our rentals you do not need to bring a Spanish licence — we operate under our commercial permit and provide the briefing required by Spanish maritime authorities. You'll need ID, be 18 or older, and complete a 10-minute on-the-dock briefing.

Where does the jet ski leave from?

Puerto Banús marina — the same pontoon as our yacht fleet. Arrive 15 minutes before your slot for the safety briefing.

Can I take a passenger?

Yes — every Sea-Doo we operate is rated for two riders. No extra cost for a passenger. Both rider and passenger get life jackets and are covered by the rental insurance.

What's included with the Mangusta 80 charter?

The Mangusta 80 superyacht charter (€4,719 for 4 hours) includes a Sea-Doo jet ski free for the entire day. The jet ski lives on the swim platform and you can use it during any anchor stop.
