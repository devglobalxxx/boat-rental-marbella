Why Choose Corporate Boat Hire in Marbella

Marbella is a year-round destination with over 300 sunny days annually. The Costa del Sol's calm Mediterranean waters make it perfect for onboard meetings, team-building activities, or sunset cruises. Our boats are equipped with comfortable saloons, sun decks, and sound systems, allowing you to tailor the experience to your corporate goals. Plus, with a licensed skipper included, there's no need for a boating license.

Team Building on the Water

Swap the conference room for the open sea. Our corporate boat hire packages can include water sports like paddleboarding, snorkelling, or jet skiing (on selected yachts). Anchor at Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés for a swim break. The relaxed atmosphere encourages natural networking and team bonding without the formality of a hotel event.

Client Entertainment Ideas

Impress clients with a sunset cruise along the Golden Mile, passing landmarks like La Concha mountain and the Puerto Banús marina. Serve cava and canapés on the foredeck, or host a private dinner on board. For a truly memorable experience, charter the Ferretti 94 (29m) with its spacious saloon and sundeck. Our team can arrange catering upgrades, from sushi to grilled seafood.

What's Included in Your Charter

Popular Routes for Corporate Charters

From Puerto Banús, you can cruise east to Marbella Marina and Cabopino, or west to Estepona and Sotogrande. A typical 4-hour trip might include a stop at a secluded cove for swimming, followed by a relaxed cruise back with drinks. For a full-day event (8 hours), we recommend heading to the dunes of Cabopino or the chic port of Sotogrande.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best boat for a corporate event in Marbella?

For team days, the Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) offers a spacious flybridge and saloon. For client entertainment, the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) includes a jet ski and premium amenities. Both depart from Puerto Banús.

How much does corporate boat hire cost in Marbella?

Prices start at €749 for 2 hours on the Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39, including skipper, drinks, and snacks. Larger yachts like the Mangusta 80 cost from €4,719 for 4 hours. Contact us via WhatsApp for custom quotes.

What is included in a corporate boat charter?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). You just bring your guests and enjoy.

Can we host a meeting or presentation on board?

Yes, larger yachts like the Ferretti 94 (29m) or Canados 86 (26m) have saloons suitable for presentations. We can arrange catering and AV equipment on request. Contact us for details.

How many guests can I bring for a corporate event?

Most boats accommodate up to 12 guests, ideal for intimate team days or client meetings. The Azimut 39 holds 11, and the Speedboat (7m) holds 6. Check our fleet page for exact capacities.

What is the best time of year for corporate boat hire in Marbella?

April to October offers warm weather (20-30°C) and calm seas. June to September is peak season with longer daylight hours. Book early for summer dates.

Do you offer catering for corporate events?

Yes, we can arrange catering upgrades such as gourmet meals, sushi platters, or BBQ options on request. Standard charters include light snacks and drinks. Discuss your needs via WhatsApp.

How do I book a corporate boat hire in Marbella?

Contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, number of guests, and budget. We'll recommend the best boat from our fleet. Payment is secure, and we confirm availability within 24 hours.
