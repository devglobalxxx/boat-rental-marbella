Why Choose a Yacht Charter in Puerto Banús?

Puerto Banús is not just a marina; it’s a landmark. Located on Marbella’s Golden Mile, it hosts some of the Mediterranean’s finest superyachts and offers direct access to the Costa del Sol’s best cruising grounds. From here you can reach Cabopino in 20 minutes, Estepona in 40 minutes, or Sotogrande in an hour. The water temperature in summer averages 24°C, and the prevailing westerly winds make for comfortable sailing or motoring. With 18 boats in our fleet, you can match the vessel exactly to your group size and budget.

Popular Destinations from Puerto Banús

Your skipper will tailor the route to your preferences. Common options include:

  • Cabo Pino – a quiet beach cove 15 minutes east, with clear water for swimming.
  • Cala del Faro – a rocky inlet with a lighthouse, 20 minutes west.
  • Estepona – a charming fishing town with a long promenade, 40 minutes west.
  • Sotogrande – a luxury marina and golf resort, 1 hour west.
  • Marbella Marina – a quick stop for lunch or a drink, 10 minutes east.

Your skipper will advise on the best route based on weather and sea conditions. Most charters include at least one swimming stop.

Tips for a Great Day on the Water

  • Arrive 15 minutes early at the marina – your skipper will meet you at the berth.
  • Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, and a light jacket – even in summer the sea breeze can be cool.
  • If you want to swim, bring a towel and swimwear. The skipper will find a calm spot.
  • Let us know if you have any dietary requirements – we can adjust the snacks and drinks.
  • For larger groups, consider the Mangusta 80 which includes a free jet ski – a huge hit with guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the yacht charter Puerto Banús price?
