Why Choose a Weekly Yacht Rental Marbella?

A weekly charter lets you slow down and truly absorb the coast. Instead of a rushed afternoon, you can wake up in a different bay each day, dine at beach clubs like Playa Padre or Nikki Beach, and anchor overnight in calm spots. With fuel and crew included, there’s no stress—just Mediterranean living. Our boats depart from Puerto Banús, the heart of Marbella’s yachting scene, giving you instant access to the Golden Mile and beyond.

Sample 7-Day Itinerary for Weekly Yacht Rental Marbella

Here’s a flexible plan that mixes exploration and relaxation:

  • Day 1: Depart Puerto Banús, cruise to Marbella Marina, swim at Cala del Faro, lunch at a chiringuito.
  • Day 2: Head east to Cabopino, anchor at the dunes, visit Playa Artola.
  • Day 3: West to Estepona, explore the old town and marina, anchor at Cala Cortés.
  • Day 4: South to Sotogrande, enjoy the exclusive port and beach clubs.
  • Day 5: Chill day—sunset cruise from Puerto Banús to Río Verde, snorkel.
  • Day 6: Longer run to Gibraltar for lunch (clearance required), return via La Concha views.
  • Day 7: Final morning swim at a favourite cove, return to Puerto Banús.

Each day covers 10-20 NM, leaving plenty of time for swimming and sunbathing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a weekly yacht rental Marbella?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No extra fees—just show up and enjoy.

How much does a weekly yacht rental Marbella cost?

Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) cost €749 for 2 hours up to €2,299 for 8 hours. Mangusta 80 variants start at €4,719 for 4 hours. All other yachts are priced on request via WhatsApp.

Can I rent a yacht for a full week in Marbella?

Yes, weekly charters are available. Contact us via WhatsApp for multi-day pricing on any boat. Our fleet includes options from 5 to 12 passengers, all departing Puerto Banús.

What destinations can I visit on a weekly yacht rental?

Popular spots include Marbella Marina, Cabopino, Estepona, Sotogrande, Cala del Faro, and Cala Cortés. Each offers unique beaches, beach clubs, and anchorages within easy cruising distance.

Do I need a boating license for a weekly rental?

No, a licensed skipper is included with every charter. You relax while the captain navigates, anchors, and ensures safety. Perfect for first-timers.

What is the largest yacht available for weekly rental?

The Ferretti 94 at 29m accommodates up to 12 passengers. Also available: Canados 86 (26m), Maiora 26m, and Mangusta 80 (24m). All depart Puerto Banús.

How do I book a weekly yacht rental Marbella?

Contact us via WhatsApp for availability and pricing. We'll confirm your preferred boat, dates, and itinerary. Payment secures the booking with no hidden charges.

What is the best time of year for a weekly yacht rental?

May to October offers warm 25-30°C weather, calm seas, and long daylight hours. July and August are peak season—book early for weekly slots.
