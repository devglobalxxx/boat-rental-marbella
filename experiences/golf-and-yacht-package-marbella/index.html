Combine the best of the Costa del Sol with a golf and yacht package Marbella – a full day of championship golf followed by a luxury boat charter from Puerto Banús. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or just want a memorable day out, this combo lets you play a world-class course in the morning and cruise the Mediterranean in the afternoon. Our fleet of 17 boats departs from Puerto Banús, and all charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks – so you can focus on the fairways and the sea.

Why choose a golf and yacht package?

Marbella is one of Europe’s top golf destinations, with over 70 courses within an hour’s drive. Combining a round with a private yacht charter gives you the ultimate luxury experience. You get the green fees and the boat hire in one seamless booking, with transfers between the course and Puerto Banús easily arranged. It’s ideal for groups of 5 to 12 people – the same group can split between golfers and sun-seekers, then reunite on the yacht.

Best golf courses near Puerto Banús

The Costa del Sol is famous for its golf courses. Here are five that work perfectly with a yacht charter from Puerto Banús:

  • Real Club de Golf Las Brisas – 18 holes, par 72, designed by Robert Trent Jones Sr. 15 minutes from the marina.
  • La Cala Resort – three 18-hole courses (Campo América, Europa, Asia). 20 minutes inland.
  • Aloha Golf Club – par 72, water hazards, views of La Concha. 10 minutes away.
  • Los Naranjos Golf Club – par 72, tree-lined fairways. 10 minutes from Puerto Banús.
  • Marbella Club Golf Resort – 18 holes, par 72, with sea views. 15 minutes.

Green fees typically range from €80 to €200 per person depending on season and tee time. We can help arrange transfers to and from the marina.

Sample itinerary: morning golf, afternoon yacht

A typical day might look like this:

  • 08:30 – Tee off at Real Club de Golf Las Brisas (or course of your choice).
  • 13:00 – Lunch at the clubhouse or en route to Puerto Banús.
  • 14:00 – Board your yacht at Puerto Banús. Welcome drinks and safety briefing.
  • 14:15–17:00 – Cruise along the coast to Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés for swimming. Enjoy drinks and snacks on board.
  • 17:00 – Return to Puerto Banús. Optional: extend the charter for sunset.

This schedule works for a 3-hour charter (e.g., €749 for the Astondoa 40) or longer if you want more time at sea. All charters are flexible – let us know your preferences.

Golf green fees are additional and paid directly to the course or through us. For a full package quote, contact us via WhatsApp with your group size, preferred date, and course choice. We’ll confirm availability for both the boat and the tee time.

Tips for a perfect day

  • Book early – especially in summer, courses and boats fill up fast.
  • Bring sun protection – the Costa del Sol averages 320 sunny days a year.
  • Consider a morning boat and afternoon golf – the sea is calmer in the morning, and afternoon temperatures are milder for golf.
  • Group size matters – choose a boat that fits your party. The Azimut 39 takes 11 pax, perfect for a fourball plus partners.
  • Ask about catering – we can arrange a barbecue or paella on board for an extra fee.

Frequently asked questions

What does a typical golf and yacht package Marbella include?

A typical package includes a round of golf at a chosen course (green fees, buggy optional) and a private yacht charter from Puerto Banús. The charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and 21% VAT. Drinks and snacks are included for the duration of the charter.

Which boats are available for the yacht charter part?

Our fleet includes 17 boats departing from Puerto Banús. For small groups (up to 5 pax) choose the Dubhe or Mariah SX21. For up to 11 pax the Azimut 39 is ideal, while the Mangusta 80 (white or grey) can take 12 pax. All boats include a skipper and amenities. See our full fleet list on the homepage.

How much does a golf and yacht package cost?

Pricing depends on the boat and golf course. For example, a 2-hour charter on an Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39 costs €749, and an 8-hour charter is €2,299. A Mangusta 80 starts at €4,719 for 4 hours (includes a free jet ski). Golf green fees vary by course – typically €80–€200 per person. Contact us via WhatsApp for a custom quote.

Can we do the golf round in the morning and the yacht trip in the afternoon?

Yes, absolutely. Many guests prefer a morning tee time at courses like Real Club de Golf Las Brisas or La Cala, then head to Puerto Banús for an afternoon yacht charter from 14:00 onwards. We can also arrange the reverse if you prefer a morning cruise followed by an afternoon round.

What golf courses are near Puerto Banús?

Top courses within 15–30 minutes of Puerto Banús include Real Club de Golf Las Brisas (18 holes, par 72), La Cala Resort (three 18-hole courses), Aloha Golf Club (par 72), and Los Naranjos Golf Club (par 72). Each offers stunning views of La Concha mountain and the Mediterranean. We can help arrange transfers.

Is the yacht charter suitable for non-golfers?

Yes, the yacht charter is perfect for everyone. While golfers play a round, non-golfers can relax on board, swim at Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, or enjoy the sun deck. The charter includes a skipper, drinks, and snacks – no golf experience needed.

How do I book a golf and yacht package?

Contact us via WhatsApp or the enquiry form on our site. Tell us your group size, preferred date, and any golf course preferences. We’ll confirm availability for both the boat and the tee time, then provide a custom quote. A deposit secures the booking.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day?

We monitor weather closely. If conditions are unsafe for sailing (e.g., strong winds or storms), we’ll offer a full refund or reschedule to another date. Golf rounds can usually go ahead unless the course is closed. We’ll keep you informed at every step.
