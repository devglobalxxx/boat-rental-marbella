Marbella's white party scene is legendary, and nothing sets the tone like a pre-party yacht charter along the Costa del Sol. Whether you're heading to a club in Puerto Banús or hosting your own celebration, a white party Marbella yacht experience combines luxury, sunset views, and the iconic all-white dress code. With boats departing from Puerto Banús, you can cruise the coastline with drinks, music, and a VIP vibe before the night begins.

Why Choose a White Party Yacht Charter?

A white party yacht charter is more than just a boat ride—it's a statement. You'll glide past the Golden Mile, sip cava, and soak up the Mediterranean sun while dressed in your finest white attire. It's the perfect way to kick off an evening, whether you're celebrating a birthday, a hen party, or simply a night out in Marbella. Our charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks, so all you need to do is show up in white.

Dress Code Tips for Your White Party Yacht

Getting the dress code right is key. Here's what to wear for a white party Marbella yacht charter:

  • All-white attire: White dresses, linen shirts, trousers, or jumpsuits work best. Avoid off-white or cream—stick to pure white for the full effect.
  • Footwear: Deck shoes, sandals, or espadrilles are ideal. Avoid black soles that can mark the deck, and skip high heels (they're not practical on a boat).
  • Swimwear: Wear your swimsuit under your outfit if you plan to swim or use the jet ski (included on Tier B boats).
  • Accessories: Sunglasses, a white hat, and a light jacket for the evening breeze are smart additions.
  • No dark colours: Black, navy, or bright colours break the white party theme—keep it monochrome.

Best Routes for a White Party Cruise

Your white party Marbella yacht charter can follow several scenic routes from Puerto Banús:

  • Marbella Coastline: Cruise east past the Golden Mile and Marbella Marina, with views of La Concha mountain.
  • Cabopino: Head west to Cabopino's sandy beaches and dunes—a quieter spot for swimming.
  • Cala del Faro & Estepona: Continue west to Cala del Faro and Estepona's old town, perfect for sunset photos.
  • Sotogrande: For a longer trip, sail to Sotogrande's luxury marina (requires more time; book an 8-hour charter).

Your skipper will tailor the route to your preferences, whether you want to anchor for a swim or keep cruising with music.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a white party Marbella yacht charter?

A white party yacht charter is a pre-party cruise where guests dress in all-white attire and enjoy drinks, music, and sunset views along the Marbella coastline. It's a popular way to start a night out in Puerto Banús, combining luxury boating with the famous white party dress code.

How much does a white party yacht charter cost?

Our Tier A boats, like the Astondoa 40 and Azimut 39, start at €749 for 2 hours or €2,299 for 8 hours, including skipper, drinks, and VAT. Tier B boats like the Mangusta 80 start at €4,719 for a minimum of 4 hours, with a free jet ski included. Other boats are priced on request via WhatsApp.

What is the dress code for a white party yacht?

The dress code is all-white attire—think white dresses, linen shirts, trousers, or jumpsuits. Wear comfortable deck shoes or sandals (no black soles). Swimwear is fine under your outfit if you plan to swim. Avoid dark colours, and bring a light jacket for the evening breeze.

Which boats are best for a white party charter?

For a group of up to 12, the Mangusta 80 (24m) or Azimut 58 Flybridge are excellent choices with ample deck space. Smaller groups (up to 9) can opt for the Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39. All depart from Puerto Banús and include a skipper and drinks.

What is included in a white party yacht charter?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). You can bring your own music playlist and additional food if arranged in advance.

Where do white party yacht charters depart from?

All our white party charters depart from Puerto Banús, Marbella's iconic marina. You'll meet the crew at the port, and the boat will cruise along the Golden Mile, past Cabopino, or towards Cala del Faro and Estepona, depending on your chosen route.

Yes, we have boats accommodating up to 12 guests, such as the Mangusta 80, Ferretti 94, or Canados 86. For groups larger than 12, you can charter multiple boats or contact us via WhatsApp for custom solutions. All boats include a skipper and drinks.
