Celebrate your baby shower on a private yacht charter from Puerto Banús, Marbella. A calm day on the bay with soft drinks, snacks, and a licensed skipper means you can relax and enjoy the moment. Choose from our fleet of luxury boats, all departing from the iconic marina, and anchor in sheltered spots like Cala del Faro or off the Golden Mile. Prices start at €749 for 2 hours, including everything except decorations. No hidden costs, no stress – just a beautiful afternoon on the water.

Why a Baby Shower Yacht in Marbella?

Marbella’s coastline offers calm waters, warm sunshine, and stunning views of La Concha mountain. A private yacht gives you a peaceful, exclusive setting away from crowded restaurants. You control the itinerary, the music, and the pace. The bay is sheltered, so even guests who are not keen on boats will feel comfortable. With a licensed skipper handling navigation, you can focus on celebrating.

Our Fleet for Baby Showers

We have boats to suit groups of 5 to 12 people. For an intimate baby shower, the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) are ideal. Both are spacious, with shaded aft decks and comfortable seating. Larger groups can book the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) from €4,719 for 4 hours, which includes a free jet ski. All boats depart from Puerto Banús and are fully equipped with safety gear, sound systems, and onboard facilities.

What’s Included in Your Charter

  • Licensed skipper (English & Spanish speaking)
  • Fuel for the full charter duration
  • Water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, and cava
  • Light snacks (chips, olives, nuts)
  • Insurance and safety equipment
  • Spanish IVA (21%) – no surprises

You are welcome to bring your own cake, decorations, or additional food. Let us know in advance and we can provide a table and help set up.

Sample Itinerary: 4-Hour Baby Shower Cruise

A typical 4-hour baby shower charter might look like this:

  • 10:30 – Meet at Puerto Banús, board your yacht
  • 10:45 – Depart, head east towards Marbella Marina
  • 11:15 – Anchor at Cala del Faro for swimming and photos
  • 12:00 – Serve cava and snacks, enjoy the calm bay
  • 13:00 – Cruise back along the Golden Mile
  • 14:00 – Return to Puerto Banús

We can adjust the route to suit your preferences – longer stops, different anchorages, or a sunset timing.

Best Anchorages for a Calm Baby Shower

The Marbella coast has several sheltered spots perfect for a relaxed celebration:

  • Cala del Faro – A quiet cove east of Marbella, protected from swell, with clear water.
  • Off the Golden Mile – Calm waters with views of luxury villas and La Concha.
  • Cabopino – A sandy beach with shallow waters, good for swimming.
  • Puerto Banús entrance – Close to the marina, calm and convenient for short charters.

Your skipper will choose the best spot based on wind and sea conditions.

Frequently asked questions

What is included in a baby shower yacht charter in Marbella?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and 21% IVA. You only need to bring decorations or extra food if desired – we can arrange catering on request.

Which boat is best for a baby shower on the bay?

For a calm, intimate baby shower, the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, up to 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, up to 11 pax) are ideal. Both depart from Puerto Banús and offer comfortable seating, shade, and easy access to calm anchorages.

How much does a baby shower yacht charter cost in Marbella?

Prices start from €749 for 2 hours on Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) up to €2,299 for 8 hours. Larger boats like the Mangusta 80 cost from €4,719 for 4 hours. All prices include skipper, drinks, and snacks.

Can we bring decorations or a cake on board?

Yes, you are welcome to bring decorations, a cake, or any personal items. We recommend lightweight, non-messy decorations. Our crew can help set up a small table. Please let us know in advance so we can assist.

Where do baby shower charters depart from?

All our charters depart from Puerto Banús, Marbella. The marina is easy to reach by car or taxi, with ample parking nearby. We meet you at the boat 15 minutes before departure.

Is the sea calm enough for a baby shower?

Yes, the bay of Marbella is generally very calm, especially in the morning. Our skippers choose sheltered anchorages like Cala del Faro or off the Golden Mile to ensure a smooth, relaxing experience.

Can we cancel or reschedule if the weather is bad?

Yes, we offer free rescheduling or a full refund if the skipper deems conditions unsafe. We monitor forecasts closely and will contact you 24 hours before departure if changes are needed.

How do I book a baby shower yacht in Marbella?

Booking is easy: contact us via WhatsApp or our website. Provide your preferred date, group size, and any special requests. We will confirm availability and send a payment link. A deposit secures the slot.
