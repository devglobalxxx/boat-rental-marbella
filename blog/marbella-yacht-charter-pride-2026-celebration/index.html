Marbella Pride 2026 is set to be the Costa del Sol’s most vibrant celebration of diversity, and there’s no better way to mark the occasion than with a private yacht charter from Puerto Banús. Whether you’re planning a flotilla with friends or an intimate party, our fleet offers boats from 6.4m to 29m, accommodating 5 to 12 guests. Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks — so you can focus on the fun. Explore our full range on the Boat Rental Marbella.

Why Choose a Yacht Charter for Marbella Pride 2026?

Marbella Pride brings thousands of visitors to the Golden Mile, Puerto Banús, and the old town. A yacht charter lets you escape the crowds while still being part of the action. Cruise along the coast, anchor at Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, and return to the marina for the evening’s events. With our boats, you control the itinerary.

Our Fleet for Pride Celebrations

We have a range of motor yachts and speedboats suitable for Pride parties. Here are the highlights:

  • Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) – From €749/2h, ideal for small groups.
  • Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) – Same price tier, more capacity.
  • Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) – €4,719 minimum 4h, includes free jet ski.
  • Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax) – Our flagship, pricing on request.
  • Fairline Targa 12m (12m, 10 pax) – Sporty and stylish.

All boats depart from Puerto Banús. For the full list, visit our fleet page.

Pricing & Inclusions

Our transparent pricing means no surprises. Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) cost €749/2h, €1,499/4h, or €2,299/8h. The Mangusta 80 (and its white/grey variants) is €4,719 for a minimum of 4 hours, with a jet ski included. All other boats are priced on request via WhatsApp.

Every charter includes: licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No hidden extras.

Itinerary Ideas for Pride Day

Start at Puerto Banús, head east towards Marbella Marina, then anchor at Cala del Faro for swimming and photos. Alternatively, cruise west to Cabopino or Estepona. For a longer trip, Sotogrande is about 30 NM away. Your skipper will tailor the route to your preferences.

What to Bring for Your Pride Charter

Bring swimwear, sunscreen (SPF 50+), sunglasses, and a hat. You’re welcome to bring your own decorations, rainbow flags, and a Bluetooth playlist. We provide towels and snorkelling gear on request. The average sea temperature in late June is 22°C.

Booking Your Marbella Pride 2026 Yacht Charter

To secure your preferred boat, book early. For fixed-price boats, use the online booking system. For others, contact us via WhatsApp. We recommend the Mangusta 80 for large groups wanting a jet ski, or the Azimut 39 for a budget-friendly option. Check our blog for updates.

Frequently asked questions

Can I book a yacht charter for Marbella Pride 2026?

Yes, you can book any of our fleet for Marbella Pride 2026. We offer boats from 6.4m to 29m, departing from Puerto Banús. Prices start at €749 for 2 hours on an Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39. All charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks.

How many people can join a Marbella Pride yacht charter?

Our boats accommodate between 5 and 12 guests. For example, the Dubhe (8m) takes 5 guests, while the Ferretti 94 (29m) holds 12. Check individual boat pages for exact capacities.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, insurance, Spanish IVA (21%), water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and light snacks. No hidden costs.

Do you offer longer charters for Pride celebrations?

Yes, we offer half-day (4h) and full-day (8h) charters. For example, Tier A boats cost €1,499/4h or €2,299/8h. The Mangusta 80 has a minimum 4-hour booking at €4,719.

Can I bring my own decorations or music for Pride?

You are welcome to bring decorations and your own playlist. Our boats have Bluetooth speakers. Please let us know in advance so we can accommodate.

Is there a jet ski available on any boat?

Yes, the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) includes a free jet ski. This is a popular option for Pride parties.

How do I book a yacht for Marbella Pride 2026?

For boats with fixed prices, you can book directly on our website. For other boats, contact us via WhatsApp for availability and pricing. Early booking is recommended.

What is the best time of day for a Pride charter?

Afternoon charters (e.g., 2–6 PM) are popular for sunset views. Morning and full-day options are also available. The weather in late June averages 28–30°C.
